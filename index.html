MTR

Repackaging company
Specialized in online retailing since 2018

Who we are ?

We are MTR a repackaging company based in North-East of France offering to our customers several services to help them in their business.

Close to the borders with Luxembourg, Germany and the Netherlands,
our geographical position makes us easily accessible to all your suppliers in Eastern Europe and the Benelux.

What services do we offer ?

A receiving service

We offer a receiving service for your B2B orders, as well as a repackaging service for your goods.

An ordering service

Allowing you to order from certain suppliers who only want to deal with companies registered in Europe for example.

A transport service

We can also transport your goods from your suppliers to our premises, we are able to provide you with a customized transport solution reducing the lead time of your supply chain.

Who are our customers ?

Our customers are usually Amazon's partner sellers who ask us to order their goods from their suppliers, then we receive and repackage them to send them to Amazon's distribution centers but we are quite flexible on our offer and we can adapt to different situations.
